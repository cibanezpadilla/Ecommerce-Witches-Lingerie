ABOUT

Witches lingerie puts health first, knowing that millions of us suffer from infections due to toxins found in and on our undies. Instead, the team uses certified organic cotton that's free from toxic chemicals and carcinogens for its range of thongs, bikinis, briefs, and bralettes. Our editor swears by the supportive keyhole bra (no underwires, and still all the oomph!). And don't even get us started on the high-rise brief—we wear it straight out of the clean laundry basket, it's that comfy.
We're super picky about who we work with because we believe quality products come from quality people who manage their farms, factories and facilities responsibly. We proudly partner with manufacturers who are aligned with our values, and rely on third-party certifications to verify our claims (GOTS and Fair Trade, to name a couple). Take a peek behind the seams to meet the people and places that make Witches sustainable from the start.
